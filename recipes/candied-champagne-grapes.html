Candied Champagne Grapes

Home

Photo by Peyton Beckwith

Description

I heard if you eat these under a table at midnight on New Years your wishes will come true.

Ingredients

  • 1 bag seedless green grapes, rinsed
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 bottle cheap Champagne prosecco

Steps

  1. Pour champagne over grapes in a large bowl just until covered, Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 12 hours.
  2. Drain grapes; return to the bowl. Toss grapes in sugar, then spread out onto a baking sheet. Freeze for at least 2 hours before serving.
  3. Serve immediately or add a few to a glass of champagne for the holidays.
